veggie burgers


if you want meatless burgers, but cannot afford Beyond/Impossible burgers, you will want to try these veggie burgers.


ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion peeled and diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced and peeled
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon coarse salt
  • ¼ fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 14 ounce can of black beans, drained & rinsed
  • ½ cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 tablespoon fine chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3 tablespoons safflower oil
  • a bun
  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium saute pan over medium-low heat.
  2. Saute the onion, garlic, and cumin together until softened and fragant, about 7 min.
  3. Add the salt,pepper,beans and rice and cook, stirring, for 7 minutes
  4. Add the cilantro and mash the mixer with a potato masher just to combine and get some coheshion- you want the burgers to have texture!!!
  5. Let the mixture cool until it is easy to handle.
  6. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  7. Put the burgers in the fridge for a few hours.
  8. Dust both sides with flour
  9. Heat the safflower oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and brown the burgers on both sides, about a minute on both sides.
  10. Serve on the buns with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
