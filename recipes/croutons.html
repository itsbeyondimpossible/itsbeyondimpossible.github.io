Croutons from bread crusts


Many people don't like bread crusts, so I made a recipe for croutons from bread crusts!


ingredients

  • bread crusts
  • olive oil
  • spices & seasoning & stuff
  1. put the olive oil in the bread crusts
  2. put the spices & seasoning & stuff on the bread before the olive oil sinks in
  3. chop up the bread crusts into about an inch-sized pieces
  4. cook the croutons in the oven until golden-brown
