what to do with bread crusts


Often you will have bread crusts and don't want to eat them but don't want to waste the bread crusts so here are some things you can do with them



  1. Eat them!

    2. probably if you are reading this, you will not want to do this


  2. make croutons
  3. make bread crumbs (you can put these on top of mac n' cheese)
  4. feed birds the crust
  5. bread pudding
  6. dump a bunch of peanut butter and/or jelly/jam on it and you will not know the difference
  7. use as a pie crust (won't work for some types of bread)
  8. dip in soups
  9. use as a thickener in a soup
